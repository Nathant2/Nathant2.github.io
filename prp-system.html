About the PRP Management System

The PRP Management System is a tool designed for the UNC-Chapel Hill Computer Science department to help manage the Preliminary Research Presentations (PRP) process for PhD students. The system streamlines scheduling, feedback collection, and tracking presentation results, providing a centralized platform for both students and faculty.

This project was developed using MongoDB, JavaScript, and Node.js, focusing on data management, secure user access, and ease of use for administrative staff and faculty members.
